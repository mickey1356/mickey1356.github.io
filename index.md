Hello!

I am currently a PhD student at the [Computational Design Lab](https://cdl.ethz.ch/) at ETH Zürich, under the supervision of [Prof. Bernd Bickel](http://berndbickel.com).

My research is focused on computational acoustics, specifically making acoustic simulations *differentiable* to aid in inverse design problems. I also have a passing interest in computational puzzle design.

Before moving to Zürich, I spent about a year as a PhD student at the [Institute of Science and Technology, Austria](https://ist.ac.at/en/home/) where I was also working under Prof. Bernd Bickel.

Previously, I graduated from [SUTD](https://www.sutd.edu.sg), and was working as a Research Engineer for 1 year at [NetVirta](https://www.netvirta.com/).
